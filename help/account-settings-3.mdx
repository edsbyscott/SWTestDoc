Students can access their Settings by clicking their profile in the top right corner of the browser navigation bar and selecting Settings.


<Frame>
![](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/student-browser-settings.png)
</Frame>

Or, on a mobile device, click Settings on the Home Screen.

<Frame>
[![](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/35-Edsby-Settings-Overview-snap-edit.png)](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/35-Edsby-Settings-Overview-snap-edit.png)
</Frame>

## Login Information

View username and current password by clicking Login Information. Usernames cannot be changed.

<Frame>
[![](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/36-Edsby-Login-Information-snap-edit.png)](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/36-Edsby-Login-Information-snap-edit.png)
</Frame>

If it is incorrect or requires updating, contact the school. However, students can change their password. Type in the new password and confirm it by typing it again under Verify Password. Click Save.

The new password is effective immediately.

## Personal Information

Students can access their Personal Information by selecting this from the Settings menu. If this information is incorrect or requires updating, contact the school.

<Frame>
[![](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/38-Edsby-Personal-Information-snap-edit.png)](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/38-Edsby-Personal-Information-snap-edit.png)
</Frame>

The role for all students in Edsby is *Student.*

## Notifications

View and update Notification Settings by clicking Notifications. Here, students can choose to be notified regarding their Messages, Personal Calendar, Organization, Classes, Groups, or Portfolio.

<Frame>
[![](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/39-Edsby-Notifications-snap-edit.png)](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/39-Edsby-Notifications-snap-edit.png)
</Frame>

Click on a row to update it. [For more information on changing your Notification Settings, click here.](https://www.edsby.com/help/notifications-students-teachers/?locale=en)

## Other Settings

Students can change their language preferences by clicking Other Settings.

<Frame>
[![](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/40-Edsby-Other-Settings-snap-edit.png)](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/40-Edsby-Other-Settings-snap-edit.png)
</Frame>

<Steps>
<Step>
Select the preferred language using the drop-down menu. Click Save. [For more information on language settings and translating in Edsby, click here.](https://www.edsby.com/help/language-settings-and-translating-in-edsby/?locale=en#:~:text=In%20the%20row%20next%20to,exit%20the%20Account%20Settings%20menu.)
</Step>
<Step>
Students can also change the theme of the Edsby Interface by using the drop-down menu under User Interface Theme. Try out Dark Mode for a fun twist!
</Step>
</Steps>
