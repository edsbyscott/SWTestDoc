---
title : "Forgotten Passwords"
description : "If a parent forgets their password, they should click “Forgot password?” on their Edsby login screen and follow the instructions on the screen."
---


<Frame>
![](https://www.edsby.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/forgot-password-1.png)
</Frame>

In some cases, a parent may not have access to the email associated with their Edsby account or the Forgot Password button may be unavailable. In this case, teachers, the principal, guidance counselors, and any IT staff can reset parent passwords.
